UkulelesEtc Workshop

UkulelesEtc Workshop we build and restore musical instruments.

Our workshops are in an old stone chapel near St. Someone's University.

The chapel was built in the early 1900s and made from granite that was cut from a quarry in [location].

Bert the Builder

When my grandfather bought it in the sixties it had an old pipe organ inside. He took on their restoration in what my mother called a 'mid-life crisis'.

But my great grandfather is a piano tuner and musician who collects and restores [old vintage period antique] pianos and organs, and specializes in pipe organs.

My grandfather restored the chapel and the pipe organ to their original splendor.

He says the organ is as much a part of the church as the church is a part of the organ.

Though, a lot of the restoration work came from local artisans and was able to be completed through the generous contributions of friends of the project.
