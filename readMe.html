What is Assignment #3

Assignment #3 is the third assignment and final project for the University of Toronto's Writing Digital Content (SCS_3681_031) course, as taught by Steve Slaunwhite.

What are the Assignment #3 Requirements?

Assignment 3 has three requirements:

  1. A persona that represents the primary target audience.
  2. A brief outline, in bullet point format, of each of the key pages.
  3. Complete content (all the writing and suggested images) for either the home page or one inside page.

The complete Assignment #3 details can be read here.
